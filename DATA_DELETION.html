Data Deletion

Walleon

You can request deletion of your Walleon app data without necessarily closing your full account.

What Can Be Deleted

  • Expenses and income records
  • Transactions
  • Wallets and linked payment method metadata
  • Budgets and spending categories
  • Upcoming bills and recurring entries
  • Profile information

How to Request

Email support@tarseedapp.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your Walleon account.
  • A description of the specific data you want deleted.

Requests are processed after we verify account ownership. Verified requests are completed within 30 days.

Note: Certain data may be retained for a limited period as required by applicable law or for fraud prevention and security purposes.

Full Account Deletion

To close your entire Walleon account and remove all associated data, visit our Account Deletion page.
