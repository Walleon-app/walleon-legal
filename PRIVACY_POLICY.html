Privacy Policy — Masrof

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

Masrof ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: email address, display name, and username, collected when you register or sign in.
  • Financial data: expenses, income, budgets, bills, and categories you create inside the app. This data belongs to you.
  • Profile photo: uploaded at your request and stored in Firebase Storage.
  • Crash reports: anonymous technical diagnostics sent to Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.

How We Store Your Data

  • On your device: all financial data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database (Drift).
  • In the cloud: your data is synced to Google Firebase Firestore so you can access it across multiple devices. Only you can read or write your own data — Firestore security rules enforce strict per-user isolation.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Crashlytics)
    Privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

We Do Not

  • Sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties.
  • Use your financial data for advertising.
  • Store your data on our own servers.

Data Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from Profile → Account Info. Deleting your account permanently removes all your data from Firestore, Firebase Storage, and your local device.
