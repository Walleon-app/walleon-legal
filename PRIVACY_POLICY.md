# Privacy Policy — Walleon
> Last Updated: May 20, 2026

Walleon ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.
This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

---

## Data We Collect

- **Account information:** email address, display name, and username,
  collected when you register or sign in.
- **Google Sign-In:** if you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your
  name, email address, and profile photo from Google. We do not receive your
  Google password.
- **Financial data:** expenses, income, budgets, bills, and categories
  you create inside the app. This data belongs to you.
- **Wallet & card tracking data:** saved payment method entries (card nickname,
  masked card number last 4 digits, expiry date) that you add for personal
  tracking. Walleon does not process or transmit raw card numbers.
- **Profile photo:** uploaded at your request and stored in Firebase Storage.
- **Usage analytics:** anonymised in-app usage data collected via Firebase
  Analytics to help us improve the app.
- **Crash reports:** anonymous technical diagnostics sent to Firebase
  Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.

---

## How We Store Your Data

- **On your device:** all financial data is stored locally in an encrypted
  SQLite database (Drift).
- **In the cloud:** your data is synced to Google Firebase Firestore so you
  can access it across multiple devices. Only you can read or write your
  own data — Firestore security rules enforce strict per-user isolation.

---

## Biometric Authentication

Walleon may offer biometric lock/unlock (Face ID, Touch ID, Android Biometrics)
as a convenience feature. Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your
device's operating system. Walleon does not receive, process, or store any raw
biometric data.

---

## Data Export

You may export your financial data as a CSV file directly from the app.
Exported files are saved to your device only and are not transmitted to any
server by Walleon.

---

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Firebase** (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Crashlytics, Analytics)
  Privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Google Sign-In**
  Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## We Do Not

- Sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties.
- Use your financial data for advertising.
- Store your data on our own servers.
- Receive or store raw biometric data.
- Issue payment cards or process financial transactions.

---

## Data Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from **Profile → Account Info**.
Deleting your account permanently removes all your data from Firestore,
Firebase Storage, and your local device.
