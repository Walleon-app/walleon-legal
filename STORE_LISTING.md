### Full Description
```
Take control of your finances with Walleon — a clean, fast, and
beautifully designed personal finance app built for everyday use.

TRACK EVERY DIRHAM, DOLLAR, OR DINAR
Add expenses and income in seconds. Organize transactions by custom
categories with your own icons and colors. Search, filter, and
review your full history anytime.

BUDGETS THAT ACTUALLY WORK
Set monthly spending limits per category and an overall monthly cap.
Watch real-time progress bars fill up and get visual alerts before
you overspend. Review past months in Budget History.

NEVER MISS A RECURRING PAYMENT
Mark any transaction as recurring — daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly.
Walleon auto-generates it for you on the due date. Preview your next
30 days of expected transactions right on the Home screen.

MANAGE UPCOMING BILLS
Add your bills with one-time, monthly, or annual recurrence. See
exactly what's due and when, with category icons for instant recognition.

INSIGHTFUL STATISTICS
Interactive charts break down your spending by category, day, week,
month, or year. Understand where your money goes with one glance.

EXPORT YOUR DATA
Export any date range to a clean CSV file and share it instantly —
perfect for bookkeeping or your own spreadsheets.

SECURE BY DESIGN
- Face ID / Touch ID biometric lock
- App Lock on background resume
- Strict per-user data isolation in the cloud
- All data synced securely via Firebase

BUILT FOR ARABIC & ENGLISH
Full right-to-left Arabic support with a beautiful localized interface.
Switch languages instantly from your profile.

DARK MODE INCLUDED
A premium dark theme that's easy on the eyes, day or night.
```
